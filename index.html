About Me

My name is Ashwini Sandanayake and I am a sophomore at the University of Minnesota studying Graphic Design. I am a second-generation Asian American who loves creating, skating, biking, and exploring. I work best with my hands, learning about new technologies. I am particularly interested in screen printing and print media and I've always had a passion for bringing my designs to life. My artwork is currently up for sale and I also do custom made-to-order prints upon request.
